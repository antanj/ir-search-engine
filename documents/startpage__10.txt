					Description:
					Rocket Science is a branding concept for B2B (business-to-business) digital marketing agency, made of five spacemen who work on launching different types of rockets in a single space station: from idea craft to design, website, video, copywriting and digital marketing.
Rocket Science branding concept is related to space, astronauts, rockets and ideas. It is neither too serious nor too deep – Rocket Science reminds of fun comic book, easy going dude, cute geek and sweet tangerine lemonade. It is fuzzy and fresh, nice to have on a sunny Tuesday afternoon. It is always trustable, a bit weird, but definitely knows his thing.
Rocket Science is combined of 5 such dudes (here a girl is also a dude) who like to work on their matters.
For someone who finds digital ideas real rocket science, this is exactly what they do.
